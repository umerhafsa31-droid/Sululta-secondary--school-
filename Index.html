sululta secondary school is a public high school located in sululta,north of Addis Abeba. the
school is provides education for students from grade 9-12,helping them prepare for
national exams and higher education. students learn important subjects such as:mathematics,
physics,English,biology,chemistry and social sciences in a supprtive learning enviroment.the
school serves both urban and rural communities, giving many young people the opportunity to
continue their education and achieve their goals.although the school faces some challenges like
limited resources,the teachers are dedicated and work hard to support their students. overall
sululta secondary school plays an important role in shaping the future of its students and
contributing to the development of the community.
